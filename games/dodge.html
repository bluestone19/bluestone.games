Dodge!

Joe is spitting bombs, and Mike's gotta dodge 'em!

BBS Post

Controls & Gameplay

  • Left & Right Arrow Keys: Move
  • Z while moving to dash
  • Dash Upgrade (double arrows): Makes your dash go further
  • Pause: Briefly pauses the debris in mid-air when you dash
  • Heart: Gives you an extra hit before you lose
  • Stopwatch: Slows down the debris for a few seconds
  • Shield: Protects you from everything for a little while
  • Multiplier: Doubles the amount of points you get from everything!
  • Passing under debris or collecting items will earn points.

Developer Notes

The idea for this one was from back when I was messing with a piece of software called Stencyl, which let you make flash games pretty easily. Me and my sister wanted to make a simple game, so our idea was this. She designed the characters.

Actually, I think the idea for this dates back further to me and my friends' Minecraft server... I tried to make an arcade machine with a redstone lamp screen where you dodge falling things. I think that came first, but I really can't be sure.
