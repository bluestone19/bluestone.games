Short Circuit

Your power plant is under attack!

Original BBS post

Controls & Gameplay

  • Arrow Keys: Move
  • Z or C: Select/Swap
  • X or V: Cancel

Developer Notes

I wanted to make a simple tile-swapping puzzle game, kinda like puzzle league. So I made this. I thought the way the tiles lit up was kinda cool, but I don't think anyone else really cared for its fairly slow controls. This badly needed some mouse support or something.
