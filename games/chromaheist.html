Chroma-Heist

Steal the colors and end the world.

Controls & Gameplay

  • Arrow Keys: Move or Select Item
  • Z or C: Open Inventory
  • X or V: Collect/Use Item
  • Collect all four chroma-prisms to gain entry to the central castle.
  • Along your way, you'll need various tools, and they will only be useful once exposed to the matching element.
  • Use the noxious gas to clean the rust off of the axe.
  • Use the water to fill the empty jar.
  • Use the lightning fields to charge the remote.
  • Use the fire to light the torch.

Developer Notes

I wanted to make a third and final "outline" game, finishing the "series" that The Untitled Experiment and Bluestone's Awakening were "in." The idea is that there'd be four colors, controlled by artifacts that you had to steal to gain their power.

This was sitting in my folder as pretty much nothing more than 25% of a map and a vague concept. It wasn't really worth posting in that state, since there wasn't even anything to play. So, I just decided to finish it. It's pretty much just "dad" but with a more definitive ending.
