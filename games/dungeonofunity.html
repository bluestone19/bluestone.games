Dungeon of Unity

Calm down the creatures so you can rescue them from the dungeon.

Itch.io page

Controls & Gameplay

  • Arrow Keys: Move
  • Z or C: Start game, collect nearby creatures, deposit creatures at rescue tiles
  • The goal is to find all of the creatures running around the dungeon and collect them. Once you collect them, you then need to deposit them at the white rescue tiles.

Developer Notes

This game's original plan had a bit more substance than what actually got made. The idea was that each creature had a different elemental attack. You could use the special attacks of creatures you collected to negate the attacks of the panicking creatures, or to capture or move them. Of course this was made for a game jam, so I didn't have time for all that.

The jam this was made for was the Fantasy Console Jam #1 held on itch.io in 2017. This was my first ever jam game.
