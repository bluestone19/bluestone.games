Bluestone's Awakening

Bluestone is a little blob that's asleep. Now he's gotta wake up by delivering presents to his friends.

Itch.io page

Controls & Gameplay

  • Left & Right Arrow Keys: Move
  • Down Arrow Key: Crouch
  • Z or C: Jump
  • X or V: Pick up or drop item
  • To hide from enemies, crouch while holding an item. You'll be safe while hiding, and the enemy will not be able to see or chase you.
  • Each of the characters has an item they like, but might accept something else instead.
  • If you're cornered or stuck, it might help to jump and either hide or show yourself. You can trick an enemy into running away!

Developer Notes

I wanted to make a kinda-sequel to The Untitled Experiment, and reuse Bluestone as a character from past attempted projects.
