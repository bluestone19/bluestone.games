The Amazing Bluestone

Bluestone can switch states of matter whenever he wants. Good for him

Controls & Gameplay

  • Left & Right Arrow Keys: Move
  • Z or C: Jump
  • X or V: Swap States
  • Get to the end of the levels. Your properties change as you switch states, so you have to be careful about your surroundings.
  • As a liquid, you can slip through grates.
  • As a gas, you can jump higher, but will be destroyed by fans.
  • As a solid, you can walk on grates without falling.
  • Heating or cooling elements can shift your state up or down.

Developer Notes
