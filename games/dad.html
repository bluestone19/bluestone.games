Controls & Gameplay

  • Arrow Keys: Move
  • Z or C: Menu
  • X or V: Get item, break rocks
  • Your main goal is to get to the final castle. To get there, you'll need a lot of equipment!
  • To open locked doors, you need to find keys.
  • To break red rocks, you need a toy hammer.
  • To walk in shallow water, you need floaties.
  • To break gem rocks, you need a gem hammer.
  • To walk over spikes, you need gem boots.
  • To cross turbulent water, you need a sailboat.

Developer Notes

The version I've got here is not the original I posted on the BBS, it's a version we worked on a bit more with slightly more tolerable music and new sprites.

The original was first made pretty soon after I was introduced to Pico-8, and Lucas wanted to make something too. It was also in the summer, and he likes Minecraft, so that's the explanation for the floaties and gem tools.

Originally, the game was supposed to have turn based battles or something but we never bothered to make that. That's what those random "enemies" in the grass were supposed to be for.
