About Using My Stuff

Feel free to send me an email and ask, if you want to use my work in your own projects. Just let me know what you're working on and I'll probably send back the OK.

  • I will never allow my work to be used in or associated with any kind of blockchain project. I'm absolutely sick of seeing that junk, and I want nothing to do with it.
  • If you want to reuse my code (don't know why you would, it's pretty bad) that's all good. Just don't make it too obvious, clean it up or edit it a bit to fit your needs.
  • If you want to use my work in something you're selling, make sure to check with me. I usually won't have a problem with it, as long as it isn't Crypto.
  • Wanna remake or port one of my games? That'd be really cool if you did.
  • This page isn't giving you permission for anything and isn't a license. Again, just email me. Please. I like getting emails.
